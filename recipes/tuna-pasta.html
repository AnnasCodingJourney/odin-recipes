Quick tuna pasta recipe

Home

Description

In a hurry ?

Try this super quick dish. Ready in 10 minutes and with only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • canned tuna
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. step: Boil the pasta
  2. step: Serve the dish

Step 1- Boil the pasta

In a pot, bring the water to boil.

Add salt and the past and cook for 9 minutes

After the pasta is cooked, remove the water in from the pasta.

Add the tuna with a bit of olive oul in the pot with the pasta and reheat for 1 minute.

Step 2- Serve the dish

The dish is ready to be served!

Serve with a bit of lemon juice and add a bit of pepper.

Enjoy!
