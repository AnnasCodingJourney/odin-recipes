Easy chocolate cake

Home

Description

This is a simple and delicious chocolate cake with chocolate frosting on top.

This very easy cake can be completed by anyone who loves chocolate.

Ingredients

  • cocao pulver
  • sugar
  • water
  • milk
  • flour
  • eggs
  • powedered sugar

Steps

  1. step: the dry ingredients
  2. step: the baking
  3. step: the frosting

Step 1- the dry ingredients :

In a large bowl, mix 2 cup of sugar, 1 tablespoon of cacao pulver, 2 cups of flour.

Stir the dry ingredients

Preheat the oven at 180 degrees.

Step 2- the baking:

Now, add the other ingredients to the bowl.

Add 1 cup of warm water, 1 cup of milk and 2 large eggs and stir.

When ready, put the mixture into a cake pan and bake it in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.

Step 3- the frosting:

Time for the frosting

Add 1 tablespoon of cacao pulver, 2 cups of powdered sugar, and 2 tablespoons of warm water and stir

When the cake is out of the oven. Let it cool down for 15min, then add the frosting on top.

Serve and enjoy!
