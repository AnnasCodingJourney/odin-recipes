Delicious veggie burger with fries

Home

Description

Are you in a mood for fries and a veggie burger ?

This delicious recipe is very easy to follow.

Ingredients

  • burger buns
  • veggie patty
  • lettuce
  • cheese (optional)
  • burger sauce
  • avocado
  • potatoes
  • salt
  • oregano
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. step: prepare the potatoes
  2. step: prepare the burger

Step 1- prepare the potatoes

Preheat the oven at 220 degrees.

Wash and cut the potatoes into equally large fries.

Add the potatoes into a large bowl, add salt, oregano, and smoked paprika and 2 tablespoons of olive oil.

Mix everything together, until the oil and seasoning is distrubuted on the fries.

Place the fries on a baking tray with the veggie patty.

Put the tray in the oven for 20 minutes.

Tip: turn the fries and the patty after 10 minutes, to avoid them sticking on the tray.

Step 2- prepare the burger

Wash the letuce and remove some leaves.

Cut the avocado into slices.

Add some burger sauce on the bun, then add the baked patty.

Add the cheese (optional), the letuce, the avocado slices, and the burger bun on top.

Serve with the fries and enjoy!
